CC: The Clear Choice for BotNets Everywhere

by nathan on Thursday, 2007-05-17

I was working this morning and noticed that "#cc":http://wiki.creativecommons.org/IRC, our IRC channel, was particularly active. But I couldn't figure out what they were talking about. It didn't look like any conversation about licensing I'd ever seen. And then I realized: it was a botnet rental negotiation. What I especially loved is the question, "you a fed?" Presumably K_Soze is under the false impression that if a law enforcement officer answers the question dishonestly, they're guilty of entrapment.
